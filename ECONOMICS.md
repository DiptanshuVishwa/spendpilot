# Unit Economics & Funnel Assumptions

## The Lead Funnel
1. **Traffic to Landing Page:** 10,000 visitors/month (via PH, HN, SEO, outbound)
2. **Audit Completion Rate:** 15% (1,500 audits run)
3. **Email Capture Rate:** 30% of completed audits (450 qualified leads/month)

## High-Value Lead Qualification
Of the 450 leads, we assume 20% (90 startups) will trigger the `$500+/mo` savings threshold, qualifying them as prime candidates for Credex cloud credits consultation.

## Revenue Projections (Proxy)
If we refer 90 high-intent startups to Credex per month:
- **Consultation Booking Rate:** 10% (9 booked meetings)
- **Close Rate:** 33% (3 closed deals/month)

Assuming average LTV of a Credex cloud credit client is $10,000:
- **Net New Pipeline Created:** $30,000/mo
- **Implied ARR impact:** $360,000/year

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Tool Development:** Fixed one-time cost (~$5k equivalent engineering time)
- **Hosting/API Costs:** <$50/month (Vercel free tier, Supabase free tier, minimal Groq API cost)
- **Effective CAC:** Near $0 organically. If we spend $1,000/mo on targeted LinkedIn ads driving to the audit, CAC per closed deal = $333.
