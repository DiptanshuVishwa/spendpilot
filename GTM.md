# Go-To-Market Strategy (30 Days)

Our target audience consists of:
- **Startup Founders / CEOs:** Care about extending runway and cutting burn.
- **CTOs / VP Eng:** Care about tool consolidation and developer velocity.

## Launch Day
- **Product Hunt:** Launch SpendPilot as a free "AI Burn Rate Calculator". Position it as a must-have tool for teams raising their next round.
- **Hacker News:** Post a "Show HN: We built a deterministic AI spend auditor." HN hates fluffy AI, so leading with "deterministic" and "no hallucinations" will play well.

## Week 1: Community Seeding
- **Founder Discords & Slack (e.g., YC, On Deck):** "Hey folks, noticed a lot of teams paying for both Cursor and Copilot. Built a quick tool to audit your AI stack and find redundant subscriptions."
- **IndieHackers:** Share the economics and technical architecture of SpendPilot.

## Week 2-3: Cold Outreach & LinkedIn
- Scrape LinkedIn for "Engineering Manager" or "CTO" at Series A/B startups.
- **Cold message:** "Hey [Name], most eng teams I talk to are paying for overlapping AI coding tools. Built a free 60-second auditor that usually finds $500+/mo in savings. Worth a look?"

## Week 4: The Credex Partnership
- Start sending automated follow-up emails via Resend to users who saved >$500/mo.
- "You saved $1,200/mo on redundant seats. Did you know you could save another 20% on your Anthropic API bill? Let me introduce you to Credex."
